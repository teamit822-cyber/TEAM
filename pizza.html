Margherita Pizza

Ingredients For the Dough: 

    
    500g strong white flour
   1 tsp salt
   1 tsp dried yeast
   200ml warm water
   1 tbsp olive oil
   For the Sauce:
   400g passata (or crushed tomatoes)
   Fresh basil leaves
   1 clove garlic (crushed, optional)
   Salt and pepper to taste
   For Topping:
   250g fresh mozzarella cheese
   Extra virgin olive oil for drizzling

Combine flour, salt, and yeast.
Add water and oil; mix, then knead for 5 minutes. 
Cover and rest for 30+ minutes.
Mix passata, garlic, basil, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
Preheat oven to 240°C. Divide dough into two, then roll each into thin 25cm rounds.
Place dough on a preheated baking sheet, spread sauce thinly,
 top with torn mozzarella, and drizzle with olive oil.
 Bake for 8–10 minutes until the crust is golden and crispy.
 Garnish with fresh basil, slice, and serve hot.
